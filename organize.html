Please note:

Earth Day Network has released its annual Leadership Guide to help individuals and communities plan memorable Earth Day events. With suggestions from tree planting to eco-fairs, this toolkit provides a cohesive theme for the thousands of events that will call on leaders to answer step up and act on climate.

This Toolkit provides resources to help you plan your own Earth Day event. If you need additional help or resources, don’t hesitate to send us an email, info@earthday.net. Once you figure out your plan, let us know and we’ll help you spread the word. The bigger the story we can tell on Earth Day 2015, the larger impact we will have.
