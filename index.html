A change needs to be made.

For many, climate change seems like a remote problem, but the reality is that it’s already affecting people, animals and places around the world.
Let 2015 be the year in which economic growth and sustainability join hands. The year in which citizens and organizations divest from fossil fuels and put their money into renewable energy solutions. The year that we utilize solutions for poverty eradication.
On Earth Day we will take a stand so that together, we can show the world a new direction.
